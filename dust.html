More About the Interstellar Dust

Img 4

As previously stated, the interstellar dust cloud consisted of rubble from the solar system that existed before our own. It was originally not particularly dense but because of the shockwave from the supernova it collapsed. The Oort cloud, named after astronomer Jan Oort, is believed to surround our solar system like a cloud. The Oort cloud is theorized to be the remnants of the original cloud of interstellar dust. More specific information on the dust cloud can be found on the Sun page or the Planet page.
