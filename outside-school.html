Outside of School

Outside of school I dance Hula with my mother and I play baskteball with a team. I dance Hula at a studio in the Lower East Side. Before actually dancing Hula, I knew close to nothing about Hawaiian culture and what actual Hula dance is. Partcipating in a dance form where culture and language is so closely tied together has been a new experience for me. In the past I did dance classes at Alvin Ailey where I learned Ballet and Modern dance. The importance of language and claiming one's culture is also a prevalent theme in Hula.
