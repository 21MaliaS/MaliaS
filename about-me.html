About Me

My name is Malia Sardinha, I am 16 and an 11th-grade student at LREI. I have a cat named Dexter, whom I love very much. He's going to be a year old in 3 months. At LREI, I participate in the x-block's Black Student Union and the Asian Affinity group. I am a co-leader the Feminism x-block and the Student of Color x-block. Outside of school, I dance Hula with my mom on Sundays. We don't always go every Sunday, but my mom always tries to go. Dancing hula has allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of the connection between imperialism, resistance, culture, celebration, and language. Aside from Hula, I used to do basketball, but I was not able to go that much because of my schedule. This November, I did crew for the LREI highschool for the first time. Doing crew for The Crucible was really fun and I learned a lot. I am designing this web site for my web design class. I've learned a lot about how people interact with web pages and the internet, which is something I had never thought about before. This class I learned how to code this webpage, as well as a lot of other pages. My favorite webpage I coded, other than this one, is my career page.
